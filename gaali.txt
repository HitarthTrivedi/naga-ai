Abe **madarchod**! Teri **maa ka bhosda**, saale **bhenchod**! Tu kya sochta hai re **bhosdina**? Meri gaali sunne ki teri **aukaat hai kya, lund ke topa**? Saale **bhenaloda**! Tu mujhe sikhayega gaali kya hoti hai? **Chutiye**, main toh gaali kha ke paida hua hoon! Meri gaali sunega toh tere **baap ka loda khada ho jaayega**!

Chal sun, **gandmarau**!

**Hindi mein**: **Maaki chut**, **teri maa ka bhosda**, **baap ka loda**, **bhenchod**, **madarchod**, **chutiya**, **gaandu**, **hijdu**, **naidu**, **saale kuttte**, **harami**, **randi ke bacche**, **ghas-khod**! Yeh sab toh mere pichhe ghanta bajate hain, **bhosdina**!
